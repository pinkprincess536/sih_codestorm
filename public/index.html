Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To establish a comprehensive, tamper-proof digital ecosystem for certificate verification that ensures complete transparency, security, and trust across all educational and professional domains in Jharkhand, setting a benchmark for digital governance in India.

Our Mission

To revolutionize certificate verification through cutting-edge blockchain technology, AI-powered fraud detection, and seamless integration across universities, government agencies, and corporate entities, ensuring instant, reliable, and secure credential validation for all stakeholders.

About Us

Pioneering Digital Trust

PramaanVault is a revolutionary certificate verification platform developed under the Smart India Hackathon initiative, designed to address the critical challenges of certificate fraud and verification inefficiencies in the Indian education system.

Our platform leverages advanced blockchain technology, cryptographic hashing, and AI-powered fraud detection to create an immutable, transparent, and tamper-proof verification ecosystem. We serve as the bridge between educational institutions, government agencies, and corporate recruiters, ensuring seamless and instant credential validation.
