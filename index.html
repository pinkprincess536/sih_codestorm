Welcome to Jharkhand Certificate Verification System

The Government of Jharkhand's Certificate Verification System provides a secure, efficient, and reliable platform for validating educational and government-issued certificates. Our advanced verification technology ensures the authenticity of documents while preventing fraudulent activities. This portal enables authorized entities including employers, educational institutions, and government departments to verify certificates issued by the Government of Jharkhand.

Companies and organizations can submit enrollment requests to get access to our comprehensive verification system. Our database includes certificates from all government educational boards, universities, and training institutes across Jharkhand. The system is designed to streamline the hiring process for employers and reduce administrative overhead for educational institutions.
